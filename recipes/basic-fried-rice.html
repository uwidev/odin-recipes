Basic Fried Rice

A fundamentals to fried rice. The context is a home kitchen and assumes a rather small pan and a somewhat underpowered stovetop.

It's important to not overcrowd your pan. At any time, only about 70% should be covered. If you go over that, work in batches.

Ingredients

  • Medium white rice, slightly with less water than usual, or fridge-chilled rice.
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Aromatics, like garlic, green onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mince aromatics.
  2. Break eggs into bowl. Salt and scrable thourougly.
  3. Oil in pan, preheat on medium-high until shimmering and hot.
  4. Eggs into pan, cook and break into small chunks. Out and reserve.
  5. Re-oil pan on medium. Wait until shimmering and hot.
  6. Aromatics in, fry until aromatic and slightly browning.
  7. Rice in, high heat. Break the rice in smaller chunks. Stir, toss, fry, until rice starts to slightly change color and turns slightly golden.
  8. Eggs in, mix.
  9. Form a well in the center. Soy sauce in. It should bubble immediately and scorch lightly. Immediately stir into the rice.
  10. Preferrably, top with green parts of green onion. Heat off and serve.
